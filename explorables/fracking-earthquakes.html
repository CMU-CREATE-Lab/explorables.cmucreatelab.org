Fracking Earthquakes

This visualization explores the link between natural gas extraction and the Guy-Greenbrier earthquake swarm. The LANDSAT timelapse footage covers the time period between 1984-2012 ans show's how the number of wells in the region increases greatly while also capturing the onset of the earthquakes in 2010.
