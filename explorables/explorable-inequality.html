Explorable Inequality: World Top Income Database

Capital in the 21st Century bases its analysis of wealth inequality on tax data from 29 countries over one hundred years. This data is openly available as The World Top Incomes Database.

For a sharable, interactive visualization of global inequality, we present this explorable viewer for the WTID. See how the income of the wealthiest classes have evolved, and how much greater wealth the top 1% have compared to the rest of society in each country. This video gives a brief introduction to the data and how to use the explorable. Use the link below to explore it yourself.
