Suburban Sprawl

This video depicts the effect of suburban sprawl near my home in Chesterfield County, Virginia. Though I did not notice the speed at which the development occurred while I lived there, when I left for college I began to realize every time I returned for the holidays that my hometown was never the same as how I left it.
