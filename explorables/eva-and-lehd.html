EVA & LEHD

This tool is focused around visualizing massive and high dimensional datasets. As an example, a sample of workforce census data has been preloaded in the system. The user can assign various dimensions of data to 5 available spatio-temporal visual dimensions and then interact with the data. The goal is to provide a tool suitable for visualizing and understanding large datasets. These stories demonstrate using EVA to explore and visualize the data from LEHD.
