Speck Air Quality Test

We arranged 144 Specks in a 12 x 12 grid to test how particles flow through the room. The Specks recorded samples once per second. The colored cells on the grid represent the values recorded. We also took periodic readings in the corners of the grid with a Hach MET ONE HHPC-6 particle counter--those readings are represented in the four corner cells labeled C1, C2, C3, and C4.
